Priyanka Halder

co-founder of Krave

Priyanka Halder is a leader with a career that spans from startups to large enterprises. She has held leadership roles at companies such as HealthEdge, Oscar Health, Tinder, and GoodRx. Halder is known for specializing in building high-performing quality teams, implementing automation strategies, and navigating complex regulatory landscapes. She holds a Master of Science in Computer Science from California State University, Long Beach, and is a passionate advocate for women in technology. Halder is also a conference speaker who shares practical strategies for fostering a quality-first culture.
