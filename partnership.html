Our Partnership with Organic Mandya

Quality You Can Trust

Our partnership with Organic Mandya ensures that every Krave roti is made with the highest quality, certified organic ingredients. Organic Mandya's commitment to sustainable farming practices aligns perfectly with our mission to provide healthy, nutritious food for families.

Why We Chose Organic Mandya

  • 100% certified organic ingredients
  • Sustainable farming practices
  • Directly supports local farmers
  • Transparent supply chain
  • Commitment to environmental responsibility

Our Shared Values

Both Krave and Organic Mandya believe in creating products that are good for both people and the planet. We're proud to work with a partner that shares our commitment to quality, sustainability, and making a positive impact in our communities.
